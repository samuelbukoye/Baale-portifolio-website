PROFFESSIONAL ACCOMPLISHMENTS

Research Efforts

For over sixteen (16) years, I have been actively involved in teaching basic and core fundamentals in Computer Science, training and mentoring students at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels. My students have been integrated into my research efforts which has resulted in a number of paper publications with them. My research focus has been in the areas of Soft Computing, Data mining, Machine Learning and User Applications. However, during my research visit to Hasso Plattner Institute, University of Potsdam, Germany, I began further research into web mining. In the course of my research over the years, I have particularly encouraged students in the design and development of application solutions to solve local problems some of which are published as journal articles and many others unpublished.
